## 🏃‍♂️ [홀수번 지문] - 🔥 우선 학습 (주전 라인업)

### 📌 [심리/과학] Unit 04 - 연습 01: 거울신경과 공감
- **핵심 키워드**: mirror neuron (거울신경), empathy (공감)
- **철수용 한 줄 요약**: "동료가 다치면 나도 찌릿하게 아픈 느낌이 들지? 우리 뇌 속에 남의 행동을 내 것처럼 비춰주는 '거울'이 있어서 그래. 팀워크의 과학적 원리야!"

> Mirror neurons are the hardware of empathy, and so what would make more sense than to look and see which animals possess these cells?
> And this is exactly where modern research now stands: all researchers know so far is that apes possess mirror neurons.
> We still need to test to see which other species are like us in this respect.
> Scientists often publicly speculate that we can probably expect surprises here, too.

### 📌 [과학/기술] Unit 04 - 연습 05: 자율주행과 정밀의학
- **핵심 키워드**: self-driving (자율주행), monitor (모니터링)
- **철수용 한 줄 요약**: "옛날엔 상상만 했던 기술이 이제 현실이 되고 있어. 자율주행차나 환자 상태를 실시간으로 체크하는 장비처럼 기술이 우리 삶을 돕고 있지."

> If you had wanted to create a "self-driving" car in the 1950s, your best option might have been to strap a brick to the accelerator.
> Yes, the vehicle would have been able to move forward on its own, but it could not slow down, stop, or turn to avoid barriers.
> But does that mean the entire concept of the self-driving car is not worth pursuing?
> No, it only means that at the time we did not yet have the tools we now possess to help enable vehicles to operate both autonomously and safely.
> This once-distant dream now seems within our reach.

### 📌 [과학/환경] Unit 04 - 연습 07: 생태계와 다양성
- **핵심 키워드**: biodiversity (생물 다양성), security (안보)
- **철수용 한 줄 요약**: "팀에 여러 포지션의 선수(다양성)가 섞여 있어야 전염병(부상)이 돌아도 팀이 무너지지 않고 버틸 수 있어. 다양성이 숲을 보호하는 안보장치야."

> Diversity provides security for ancient forests.
> Because fungi are also very dependent on stable conditions, they support other species underground and protect them from complete collapse to ensure that one species of tree doesn't manage to dominate.

### 📌 [과학/생태] Unit 05 - 연습 01: 꽃의 광고 전략
- **핵심 키워드**: language of smell (냄새의 언어), market (마케팅)
- **철수용 한 줄 요약**: "꽃들은 꿀을 파는 식당이야. 손님(곤충)을 끌어모으려고 '향기'라는 아주 강력한 광고를 내보내는 거지."

> Just like the restaurants, nature has its own dining establishments.
> Instead of using a written language or neon sign, they advertise their offerings just as effectively using the language of smell.

### 📌 [과학] Unit 05 - 연습 03: 과학적 측정과 이론
- **핵심 키워드**: measurement (측정), calculate (계산)
- **철수용 한 줄 요약**: "별의 무게를 어떻게 잴까? 직접 저울에 올릴 순 없으니, 우리가 아는 전술 공식(이론/수식)을 써서 간접적으로 계산해내는 추리 게임이야!"

> In order to "measure" the star mass, we measure other quantities and use those values, together with certain equations, to calculate the mass.

> Based on discoveries in neuroscience, pain and pleasure are formed and processed in the same area of the brain.
